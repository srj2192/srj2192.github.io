How I work

"The engineers who build things that last rarely write about it."

I'd rather be known for systems that survive on-call than slides that survive LinkedIn. My best work has always started with a blank canvas and a hard constraint — the ranking platform, the IVR system, the Semantic Service. Zero-to-one demands a different mindset: you're not optimising within constraints, you're defining them.

"AI has become the subject of my work, not just a tool within it."

At Contentful I went from someone who uses AI tools to someone who builds the infrastructure others rely on to deliver AI features. That transition — from consumer to constructor — has fundamentally changed how I approach backend engineering. Every system I design now considers the AI layer: where it belongs, how it fails, and how to keep it honest.
